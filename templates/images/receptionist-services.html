Live Reception services

  • ANNEXIS can assign live receptionist to answer your calls.
  • Packages available to fit all budgets and are scalable to support your need.
  • A reception can receive and sign for your parcels at your virtual office.
  • Live receptionist can also greet your clients as they visit you on your office.


Dedicated Agents

If you wish to have a dedicated agent to answer only your calls we can arrange to set your call routing to be directed to an agent specifically assigned to you. This live receptionist will receive calls for the business, provide direction and instructions to your customers and take information from your client whether its questions or orders.


Toll Free No 800 number

Phone calls are a vital aspect of every business these days and we consider it as a backbone of any business no matter whether you a small organization or a mid-sized firm. We provide our customers with a toll-free 800 number that will be an extra advantage for any organization.


Our Telephone Answering services offer the following benefits:
  • Local business telephone number
  • Toll free number
  • Professionally managed business call center, answering phone calls in your company's name.
  • Dedicated receptionist to receive calls, take messages or transfer calls to your personal number.
  • Calls answered by a receptionist in your company name 24/7, 365 days a year, as per your requirement.
  • During holidays or extended trips away from your business, special instructions can be given to reception with regards to the handling of your incoming calls during that period.

Video Conferencing:

All office locations are equip with state-of-the-arts communication tools to conduct remote meetings and conferences with clients and headquarter.


Fax

A fax number is also provided by ANNEXIS mainly for the business communication. This facility helps our clients in receiving fax from anywhere in the world. Incoming faxes will be forwarded to you via email.


Reliable, fast, optimized Internet & Wi-Fi access

All our Virtual offices use the best technology to make available to you the latest in high-speed Internet connection. Keeping you connected to the Internet and eliminates any lag time during web surfing and video conferencing with potential clients.
