Sales Consulting Services

ANNEXIS can also assist you to build a sales team in the US.


With ANNEXIS working for you, you will surely have return calls by individuals and companies interested in your product or services.


You now have the ability to employ and train sales representative already located in the US to quickly respond to the interest and deliver the information that was requested in a professional manner and ultimately increase your revenue.


Having this advantage put you light years ahead of a traditional business that is reaching out over seas to respond to leads.


This service is very cost effective because:

  • No need to consider various night shift at the main office because of time differentials
  • No language barrier issues because sales representatives are natives to US
  • No large travel cost to meet the interest party because sales representative are trained by you on products and service and will use available tools provided by ANNEXIS to support the sales such as conference rooms, video conference equipment, attorneys, etc..

Your dedicated sales representative will:

  • Travel and meet your potential clients anywhere in the US.
  • Generate credibility for your business by meeting with your prospective clients on your behalf, which in turn will generate revenue, impacting your bottom line.
  • Facilitate video conference meetings with you and potential or existing clients
  • Be the US extension of your company in India.
  • Take order request or services and arrange contract and payment details.
  • Training of product understanding and usage.
  • Respond to customer complaints and perform gap analysis on remediation plan and resolutions.
  • Develop and propose sales forecast and budgets.
