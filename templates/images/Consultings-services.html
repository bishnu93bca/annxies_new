Consulting Services

Sales consultants:

ANNEXIS can also assist you to build a sales team in the US. We will source and provide you with options to choose highly qualified representative that can promote your business best. The identified representative(s) assigned to your company will promote and respond to your sales opportunities and potentials. With the help of ANNEXIS hiring a sales person to promote your business in the US can now be in hours.


Your dedicated sales representative can travel and meet your potential clients anywhere in the US. Our sales rep will generate credibility for your business by meeting with your prospective clients on your behalf, which in turn will generate revenue, impacting your bottom line. Your Sales representatives can facilitate video conference meetings with you and potential or existing clients – They will be your US extension of your business in India.


As a leading consultancy with sophisticated analysis tools and experienced sales consultants, we help you determine with confidence which paths will lead to significant increases in sales performance with the help of our efficient and reliable sales consultants. You'll work with a sales consultant who knows the ins and outs of your industry, and who will work with you to uncover the changes you need to make to see dramatic sales improvement. Then, we'll help you move from recommendations to action and change.


As a leading consultancy, we've worked with hundreds of organizations around the world to help them improve sales performance.


Sales Representative

Sales representatives may do some or all of the following:

  • Communicate with prospects and convert leads to clients.
  • Take order request or services and arrange contract and payment details
  • Training of product understanding and usage
  • Respond to customer complaints and perform gap analysis on remediation plan and resolutions.
  • Develop and propose sales forecast and budgets.

We employ a personal, consultative approach to satisfying our clients' demands. Call one of our experienced, friendly consultants today to discuss your requirements.
